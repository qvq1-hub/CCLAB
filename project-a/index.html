What we know so far about [Mimi the 1st ]? Mimi the first is an incident created from a science experiment of a scientist called Mimi. This is a creature that can only thrive in a colorful environment, scientist have to put it in a box with rainbow wrappings to maintain its life. However, this is a venomous creature, it attracts humans or preys by hypnotizing them using its body movement. Humans and preys will be hypnotize, overwhelme and dizzy when looking at the the swirling in circle motion of this creature. Moreover, this creature depends on light as its main way to consume nutrition, so scientist have to feed this creature lights.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature By pressing the mouse you can feed mimi the 1st with light. However, be careful when approaching this creature, as it can hypnotize you with its swirling motion. Avoid looking directly at it for too long, as it may cause dizziness and disorientation.
